Our Accessibility Commitment

Every story on StoryCast includes:

  • Full text transcripts synchronized with media
  • Closed captions for all video content
  • Audio descriptions for visual elements
  • Keyboard-navigable media players
  • Screen reader optimized markup

We follow WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards and continuously test with assistive technologies.
